Bushwick Collective

The Bushwick Collective in Brooklyn is one of NYC's most concentrated areas for large-scale murals, featuring works by both local and international street artists. What began as a local initiative has grown into an open-air gallery spanning multiple blocks.

Artist Spotlight: Lady Pink

Sandra "Lady Pink" Fabara is an Ecuadorian-American graffiti artist who began painting on trains in the late 1970s and became one of the few women active in the male-dominated graffiti subculture.

Her work often addresses feminist themes and social issues, and can be seen throughout NYC, particularly in Queens where she maintains a studio. Notable works include the "Subway Therapy" wall in the East Village.
