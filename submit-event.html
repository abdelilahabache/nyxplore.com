Submission Guidelines

Eligibility

We feature cultural events including art exhibitions, performances, lectures, workshops, and festivals. Commercial sales events or promotional offers are not eligible.

Approval Process

All submissions are reviewed by our editorial team. Approved events typically appear within 2-3 business days. We reserve the right to edit event details for clarity.

Image Requirements

Images should be high-quality (minimum 1200px wide) and relevant to the event. By submitting images, you confirm you have rights to use them for promotion.
