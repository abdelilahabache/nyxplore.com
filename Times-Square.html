Times Square

The Crossroads of the World

The Heart of New York City

Where bright lights and Broadway meet

Times Square is one of the world's most visited tourist attractions, drawing over 50 million visitors annually. The bright lights and giant digital billboards have made it an iconic symbol of New York City.

Daytime Experience

Explore the bustling streets, visit flagship stores, and see Broadway theaters in daylight. Daytime offers better visibility for people-watching and architecture appreciation.

Nighttime Magic

Witness the transformation as neon lights illuminate the square. The energy peaks at night with street performers, live music, and the famous New Year's Eve ball drop.

Broadway Theaters

Home to 41 professional theaters with nearly 500 seats each. Catch world-class performances from classic musicals to cutting-edge plays.
