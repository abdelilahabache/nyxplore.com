What Makes NYC Pizza Special?

Before diving into our rankings, let's break down what makes New York pizza truly unique:

  • The Water: NYC's tap water is famously soft and mineral-rich, contributing to the perfect dough texture‚Äîcrispy on the outside, chewy on the inside.
  • The Ovens: Traditional coal-fired and brick ovens give NYC pizza its signature char and smoky flavor.
  • The Fold: A true New York slice is sturdy enough to fold in half‚Äîa technique that prevents toppings from sliding off.
  • The Attitude: NYC pizza is fast, no-frills, and often served with a side of attitude.
