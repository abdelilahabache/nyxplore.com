Museum Visitor Tips

Best Times to Visit

Weekday mornings are typically least crowded. Evenings at MoMA (open until 9pm on select days) offer a more relaxed experience. Avoid holiday weekends when tourist numbers peak.

Ticket Savings

Consider combo passes like the New York Pass or CityPASS for multiple museums. Many museums offer student, senior, and military discounts with valid ID.

Accessibility

All major museums offer wheelchair access, sign language tours, and sensory-friendly hours. Check museum websites for specific accommodations and programs.
