Our NYC Story

Discover the passion behind NYxplore

Who We Are

NYxplore was born from a simple idea: to create the most authentic, immersive guide to New York City. Founded by a team of lifelong New Yorkers and passionate explorers, we're dedicated to showcasing the soul of the city beyond the typical tourist spots.

Our mission is to connect people with the real New York—the hidden courtyards, the family-run eateries, the vibrant street culture, and the stories that make each neighborhood unique. We believe the best NYC experiences happen when you explore like a local.

Our Values

These principles guide everything we do at NYxplore:

Authenticity

We only recommend places we genuinely love and think you will too. No paid placements or fake reviews.

Local First

We prioritize small businesses and neighborhood institutions over tourist traps.

Accessibility

New York is for everyone. We highlight affordable options and accessible experiences.

Sustainability

We promote eco-friendly ways to explore and support businesses with green practices.

Our Story

How NYxplore grew from a passion project to NYC's most trusted travel guide:

NYxplore began as a passion project, born from a deep love for New York City. We wanted to create more than just a travel guide—we envisioned a trusted platform that captures the true spirit of NYC, crafted with care by those who know it best.

To bring this vision to life, we partnered with local experts, seasoned guides, and trusted sources to gather authentic insights, hidden gems, and must-see spots across the city. Every recommendation, tip, and itinerary is infused with local knowledge, ensuring an experience that goes beyond the typical tourist path.

Built by NYC lovers, for NYC lovers, NYxplore has become the city's most trusted travel companion. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a lifelong New Yorker, we're here to help you explore the city with confidence—and maybe even fall in love with it as much as we have.

When you use NYxplore, you can trust that:

  • We update our guides regularly to reflect changes
